PRIVACY

Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026

1. Local-First Data Storage. Odostrada is designed to keep your mileage ledger on your device. Odometer readings, segment classifications, location tags, odometer images, legal-consent records, and related settings are stored locally on the device unless and until you choose to export or share them.

2. Location Data. Odostrada requests location access only when you create an anchor and only to tag that anchor. Odostrada does not use background location tracking to monitor your movement over time. If you deny location permission, the app can still log mileage, but anchors may be saved without GPS metadata.

3. Camera and Images. Odostrada may request camera access to capture odometer images and run on-device OCR. If camera access is denied, the app can still be used by manually entering odometer readings. Odometer images are stored locally and may be included in exports or backups you choose to create.

4. No Central User Account. Odostrada does not require you to create an account with us. However, if you purchase Business Pro, subscription and entitlement status may be processed by our billing and subscription provider, RevenueCat, and the applicable app marketplace such as Google Play.

5. Third-Party Services. Odostrada uses third-party platform services where necessary to support subscriptions, payments, reminders, link handling, and map launching. For Business Pro entitlement handling, RevenueCat may process purchase and subscription metadata in accordance with its own privacy practices.

6. User-Initiated Sharing and Export. Odostrada does not automatically upload your trip history to our servers. If you export CSV files, odometer photo archives, or full backups, and then share them through email, cloud storage, messaging apps, AirDrop, or similar services, those files may leave your device and become subject to the privacy practices of those services.

7. Data Retention and Deletion. Because Odostrada is local-first, you control retention. Data generally remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the app, clear app storage, or overwrite it through restore or import actions.

8. Legal Consent Records. Odostrada may record the time, source URL, and version of your acceptance of legal terms on your device. These records may also be included in user-created backups.

9. Changes to This Policy. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When required by the app, you may be asked to review and accept updated legal terms before continuing to use certain features.
