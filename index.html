No Tracking Required.

Accuracy without the intrusion. Odostrada uses Anchor & Segment logic to give you tax logs without ever following your movement.

Anchors, Not Trips

Log your odometer at stop points. Your location is only accessed at that precise moment. Zero background tracking.

Natural Reconciliation

Local Only

Privacy Policy

Data Sovereignty: Odostrada does not maintain accounts or operate central servers. All odometer images and logs are stored exclusively on your device.

Location Processing: GPS is only accessed when you manually create an "Anchor". No background tracking or movement monitoring is performed.

Data Deletion: You have total control. To delete all data, clear the App's storage in your system settings or uninstall the application.
