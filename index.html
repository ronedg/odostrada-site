Ditch the battery-draining GPS. Odostrada uses Anchor & Segment logic to provide 100% accurate tax logs without ever monitoring your movement.

Privacy Policy

Data Minimization: We adhere to a strict policy of data minimization. The App is architected to ensure that no Personal Identifiable Information (PII), location coordinates, or images are ever transmitted to the Company or any third-party cloud providers.

Local Processing: All sensor data and odometer readings are processed and stored exclusively in a private SQLite database on your device. We have zero technical access to your logs.

GPS Usage: Location access is "On-Demand" only to establish mileage Anchors. We do not track your movement in the background.
