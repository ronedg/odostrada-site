How Odostrada Works

Odometer anchors replace constant GPS tracking. Three steps. Full control.

1. Create Anchor

Enter or photo your odometer reading on arrival. Location is requested only at that moment.

2. Calculate Trip

The next anchor computes the exact distance traveled from the prior anchor. No background route tracking required.

3. Classify & Reconcile

Tag business or personal use, split mixed-use mileage, and export organized reports for your own bookkeeping workflow.
