How Odostrada Works

Odometer anchors replace constant GPS tracking. Three steps. Full control.

1. Create Anchor

Enter or photo your odometer reading on arrival. GPS requested only at that moment.

2. Calculate Trip

Next anchor instantly computes the exact trip distance. No background location data ever collected.

3. Classify & Reconcile

Tag business/personal. Split trips for multi-stop days. Export CPA-ready reports.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026

For the purposes of this policy, "the Company" refers to Mana Kitchen LLC and its authorized DBAs.

1. Local-First Data Sovereignty. odostrada is designed with a "Zero-Server" architecture. Your mileage ledger and trip history are stored exclusively on your device. The Company does not operate a central database and cannot see or access your records.

2. Location Data. Precise Location access is requested only when you manually create an "Anchor." The Company does not engage in background tracking or persistent movement monitoring.

3. Camera & Image Capture. Camera access is used for capturing odometer images. The Company does not have access to your camera feed or resulting images; all framing and storage occur locally on your device hardware.

4. Pseudonymous Identifiers. Third-party billing services (including RevenueCat) may assign a random identifier to your device to verify "Pro" status. This identifier does not contain personal identity information (PII) accessible by the Company.

5. Data Retention & Deletion. You control all data. Deleting the app removes all local records. Because the Company does not store your data on servers, we cannot "restore" logs for you if you uninstall the app without a manual backup.

6. Children’s Privacy. The App is a business tool intended for adults. The Company does not knowingly collect or solicit data from children under the age of 16.
