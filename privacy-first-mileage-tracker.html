A Privacy-First Mileage Tracker for People Who Mean It

Most mileage trackers ask you to trade privacy for convenience. odostrada is built around a different idea: a private mileage tracker can still be fast, useful, and local-first.

No account

There is no login wall, no account setup, and no central trip history tied to you. If you want a no account mileage tracker, that matters from the first launch.

No background GPS

odostrada does not need an always-on route trace to do its job. You log odometer anchors, and the app calculates trip distance between them. Location can be requested at the moment you create an anchor, not all day long.

No server-side trip storage

Your mileage log stays on your device. If you back it up or export it, you choose the destination. odostrada does not keep your trip data on our servers, which is the whole point of a local-first mileage tracker.

Smart without surveillance

A privacy-first app does not have to be dumb. odostrada can still suggest likely vehicles, odometer readings, locations, and business purposes by using the records already stored on your device. That means you get time-saving help without handing over your movement history.
